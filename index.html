The Heart Behind Our Mission

We do this work because every cow carries a silent plea in her eyes—a plea for mercy, for safety, for the simple right to live without fear. These gentle giants, who give us milk, plow our fields, and hold sacred space in our culture, are now left to suffer in shadows. They stumble through streets chewing plastic to fill starving bellies. They stand crammed into slaughter trucks, trembling as their kin fall one by one. They are mothers whose babies are torn away, their mournful cries echoing into indifference.

This is why we rise!

Rescue

At Shree Gayatri Sevabhavi Sanstha, we are dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and providing lifelong care to abused, abandoned, and neglected cows. These gentle beings often suffer in illegal slaughterhouses, dairy farms, or as abandoned strays—enduring cruelty, malnutrition, and neglect.

Feeding

At Shree Gayatri Sevabhavi Sanstha, we believe no cow should go hungry. Many rescued, abandoned, or stray cows suffer from severe malnutrition, eating plastic or garbage to survive. Our Cow Feeding Program ensures these gentle souls get wholesome meals, clean water, and the care they deserve.

Gallery

Welcome to our cow rescue gallery, showcasing the heartwarming journey of rescued cows finding safety, care, and love at our sanctuary. Each photo tells a story of compassion, healing, and the bond between humans and animals.
